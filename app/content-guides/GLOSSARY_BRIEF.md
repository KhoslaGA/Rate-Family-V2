**Target length per term:** 4–6 sections, ~150–250 words each. 2–4 FAQs. 1–3 sources. 3–6 related terms.

### Compliance rules — STRICT

- **No invented dollar amounts** in examples. Real regulatory caps are fine (e.g., "$3,500 minor-injury cap" with FSRA/SABS citation), but worked examples that say "Aisha's $4,200 physio bill" are not.
- **No specific carrier market-share percentages** (e.g., "Intact 21.4% market share"). Reference carriers generically if at all.
- **No "average savings of X%"** style unsubstantiated stats.
- **No operator licence numbers** (e.g., "RIBO #84211"). The general "RIBO" is fine.
- **Cite FSRA, RIBO, or Ontario e-Laws** for any regulatory claim. Secondary sources (law firm blogs, insurer marketing) are OK as supporting reading but should not be the only citation for a regulatory fact.
- **Hedge where you would be guessing.** If you don't know the exact figure, describe the structure ("a monthly cap that varies by injury severity") rather than invent one.
